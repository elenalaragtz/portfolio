BUFFALO PIZZA 

By elena lara
Published on October 23, 2022

For Buffalo Pizza, as a designer, I helped design all the POP and digital marketing since the fist opening till our new 3rd location. 

I help them redesign the initial idea for the brand with the concept of duotone. Using the right applications for the restaurants, like the menu, flyers, posters, and more. 

In collaboration with the photographers, we decide to make the photos all withe so it will be easier for the post edition for social media and others applications. 

The assembly for the layout of the restaurants, helping the user to have a more present experience and a good looking picture if they wish.
