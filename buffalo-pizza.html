BUFFALO PIZZA

In collaboration with the entire team, including designers, entertainers and photographers, we managed to create a campaign for the opening of the new Buffalo Pizza branch in Plaza Anahuac.

Plaza Anahuac (NEW OPPENING)

I help them redesign the initial idea for the brand with the concept of duotone. Using the right applications for the restaurants, like the menu, flyers, posters, and more. 

In collaboration with the photographers, we decide to make the photos all white so it will be easier for the post edition for social media and others applications.
